CHAPTER 1

Foundations of B2 English

In this chapter, you will learn how to:

  • Understand CEFR B2 requirements
  • Upgrade B1 sentences to B2 level
  • Use advanced connectors confidently
  • Express ideas clearly and in detail

What Does B2 Really Mean?

At B2 level, learners are expected to communicate with confidence and clarity. This means expressing ideas in detail, supporting opinions with reasons, and using a range of vocabulary and grammar accurately.

B1: I like my job because it is interesting.
B2: I enjoy my job because it provides meaningful challenges and opportunities for professional growth.
EXAM TIP
At B2 level, avoid very basic words such as big or good. Use more precise alternatives to show vocabulary control.

Practice

Rewrite the sentences to make them B2 level.

  1. The service was slow.
  2. People use AI. It saves time.

Self-Check

  • I can write longer, clearer sentences.
  • I can support my opinions with reasons.
  • I can avoid very simple sentence patterns.
